- **Dependencies:** `matplotlib`, `numpy`, `pandas`, `pint`, `scipy`, `uncertainties`.
- **Core Functionality:**
    - Calculates specific heat capacity ($C_v$) using electrical heating.
    - Two-phase process:
        1. **Calibration:** Uses a known standard (Silver) to find the system's water equivalent ($W$).
        2. **Measurement:** Uses $W$ to find $C_v$ for unknown samples (Copper, Aluminum, Iron).
    - Performs linear regression on $\Delta t$ vs. $\Delta T$.
    - Outputs plots and a summary report.
